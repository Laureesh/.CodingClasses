About

Barry Allen, also known as The Flash, is a fictional superhero from DC Comics. Gifted with super-speed after a particle accelerator accident, he protects Central City and the multiverse from various threats. His character represents hope, resilience, and the power of science combined with humanity.

Barry Allen works as a forensic scientist for the Central City Police Department. After gaining his powers, he dedicated his life to fighting crime and discovering the mysteries of the Speed Force. His journey balances heroism, personal struggles, and relationships.
